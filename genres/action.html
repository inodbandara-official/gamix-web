Action Genre

Action video games are a subgenre of computer and video games that empha difficult physical tasks, rapid-fire action, and frequently violent content. To accomplish a particular objective, such as saving the world, helping a heroine in trouble, or finishing a task, players typically assume the character of a protagonist who must surmount numerous challenges and foes.

For these games to be successful, players usually need quick reactions, strategic reasoning, and exact timing. Many action games also let players use a variety of weaponry and unique skills to take down foes and surmount obstacles. The score, level, or capacity to accomplish increasingly challenging tasks are frequently used to gauge a player's development.
